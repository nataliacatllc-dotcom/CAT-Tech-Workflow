Workflow Decisions — Confirmed
01Quote path: All projects get a quote number. Verbal/email proposals are converted to a formal quote. Job number assigned only after customer PO is received (from job log).
02Vendor order: Quote line items mirror the Project Control in standard order — Herding, CAT Controls, Mike Pavek, JH Bennett, Spencer-Howden, Prater, HP Products, Norres, Lyle Pizzino, Lakeside Supply Co., Other (for non-standard vendors).
03Payment schedule: Invoice split varies by project and must be shown on the quote so the customer sees the pay schedule upfront.
04Drawing trigger: Approval drawings submitted 2–4 weeks after PO triggers Invoice #1 (engineering). Procurement orders placed at same time.
05Change orders: Same quote number retained. Change order scope added as new line items on the invoice — no new quote issued.
